01. About

Hello! My name is Bruno Henrique, I'm 19 years old, and I'm from São Paulo, Brazil. My journey into the world of technology began with a passion for video games. In 2019, I took my first steps into programming when I had my initial encounter with Python. However, it was only in 2022, after turning 18, that I decided to fully immerse myself, realizing that bringing websites and software to life was what fulfilled me as a person.


Currently, I'm focused on my professional growth and seeking specialization in web development. Alongside my studies, I work as a freelancer, creating websites for major companies in my region.

Regarding technologies, I'm honing my skills in React JS and exploring other libraries like Tailwind CSS to expand my knowledge and avoid stagnation in any language.
